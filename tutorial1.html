ROS 2 Humble Basics

ROS 2 Humble Hawksbill is a special software used to control robots. It's like a toolbox that helps people build robots that can do cool things, like move on their own or follow commands. With ROS 2 Humble, robots can talk to their sensors and other parts to work together better. It's used in many types of robots, from small ones to big machines. This version is also safer and faster than older ones, making it a great choice for learning how to program and control robots.
