Frequently Asked Questions

What is BOTIFY?

BOTIFY is an innovative educational platform for children that helps them learn the basics of STEM.

How do I get started with BOTIFY?

To get started, simply click the "Begin your journey" button on our homepage and follow the prompts to create an account or log in.

Is BOTIFY free to use?

BOTIFY offers both free and premium features. Basic functionality is available at no cost, while advanced features may require a subscription.

How secure is my data on BOTIFY?

We take data security very seriously. All user data is encrypted and stored securely, and we never share your personal information with third parties.
